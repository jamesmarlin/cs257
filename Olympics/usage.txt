usage: olympics.py [-h] --NOC S [S ...] --medal S [S ...] --weight S [S ...]

optional arguments:
  -h, --help            show this help message and exit
  -NOC S [S ...], --NOC S [S ...]
                        Using a S input, prints out a list of athletes from the given region
  -MEDAL S [S ...], --medal S [S ...]
                        Using a S input, prints out a list of in descending order of medals from the specified NOC
  -WEIGHT S [S ...], --weight S [S ...]
                        Using a S input, prints out a list of athletes with the specified weight

#Order of flags can be different
#You do not have to use all three arguments
